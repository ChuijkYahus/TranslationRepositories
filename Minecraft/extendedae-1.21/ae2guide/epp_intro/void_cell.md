# ME Void Cell

A pocket condenser for use in a drive.

<ItemImage id="extendedae:void_cell" scale="4"></ItemImage>

The Void Cell needs to be partitioned on <ItemLink id="ae2:cell_workbench" /> before use. It deletes everything that
matches its filter or condenses matching items into <ItemLink id="ae2:matter_ball" />s or <ItemLink id="ae2:singularity" />s like <ItemLink id="ae2:condenser" />.

Right-click it to open the configuration GUI.
