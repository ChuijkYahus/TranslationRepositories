# ME Crystal Fixer

<BlockImage id="extendedae:crystal_fixer" scale="8"></BlockImage>

ME Crystal Fixer can repair a Budding Certus Quartz block and upgrade it.

It needs a <ItemLink id="ae2:charged_certus_quartz_crystal" /> and power to work. Right-click it with <ItemLink id="ae2:charged_certus_quartz_crystal" /> to insert it.
